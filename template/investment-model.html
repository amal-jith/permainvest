Investment Philosophy

Our Core Belief

At PermaInvest, we believe that enduring value is created through control, operational excellence, and time, not financial engineering alone. We invest with a long-duration mindset, partnering with businesses where we can actively shape outcomes, compound cash flows, and build strategic platforms that scale.

Our Mandate

Acquire controlling or highly influential stakes in resilient, cash-generative businesses

Focus on platform creation, not isolated transactions

Deploy capital with a permanent or long-hold orientation

Drive value through execution, technology, and integration

Key Investment Pillars

1

  • Strategy

  • Capital allocation

  • Leadership decisions

Minority investments are considered only where structural protections and influence are equivalent to control.

2

  • Acquire at disciplined valuations (often below intrinsic value)

  • Structure transactions with asymmetric risk profiles

  • Focus on cash flow visibility and resilience

We do not rely on multiple expansion; we underwrite to cash yield and operational uplift.

3

  • Consolidate fragmented industries

  • Integrate acquisitions into unified operating models

  • Extract synergies across systems, procurement, and workforce

Each investment must have clear adjacency pathways for expansion.

4

  • Hands-on operational involvement

  • Centralised support across finance, HR, compliance, and systems

  • Deep sector execution capability

We embed execution discipline at the core of value creation.

5

  • Data infrastructure (e.g. Snowflake)

  • Workflow automation and RPA

  • Proprietary AI-driven decision tools

Technology is not an overlay, it is a core driver of margin expansion, scalability, and defensibility.

6

  • Hold assets if they continue to compound value

  • Exit only when risk-reward dynamics change materially

    • This enables

  • Better decision-making

  • Stronger management alignment

  • Sustainable long-term growth

7

  • Prioritise high-return reinvestment opportunities

  • Maintain balance sheet strength

  • Avoid over-leverage and short-term optimisation

Every dollar deployed must enhance long-term enterprise value per share.

8

  • Founder-led or operator-driven businesses

  • Management teams aligned on performance, accountability, and growth

    • We instill a culture of:

  • Ownership

  • Transparency

  • High-performance execution

Our Edge

We focus on businesses with:

  • Strong recurring or predictable cash flows

  • Fragmented markets with consolidation potential

  • Opportunities for operational uplift and margin expansion

  • Clear pathways to scale through acquisition or organic growth

  • Defensive characteristics in down cycles

Our value creation model is driven by:

  • Operational optimisation

  • Technology enablement

  • Strategic M&A and consolidation

  • Centralisation of core functions

  • Pricing and margin discipline

We do not invest in:

  • Passive minority positions without influence

  • Businesses reliant on speculative growth assumptions

  • Situations where value creation depends primarily on leverage or multiple expansion

Our value creation model is driven by:

  • Operator-first mindset with real execution capability

  • Proven ability to integrate and scale acquired businesses

  • Deep expertise in workforce, industrial, and services sectors

  • Embedded AI and data-driven decision infrastructure
