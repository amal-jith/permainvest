Investment Values

At PermaInvest, capital is a means to create enduring value — not only for investors, but for businesses, employees, communities, and the broader economy. Our investment decisions are guided by a principled framework that emphasises integrity, discipline, and long-term stewardship.

1

  • Full diligence materials shared with participating investors

  • Clear disclosure of fees, incentives, and potential conflicts

  • Straightforward reporting on performance and progress

  • No hidden structures or opaque arrangements

Transparency ensures informed decision-making and strengthens long-term relationships.

2

  • Alignment of GP and investor interests through co-investment

  • Respectful treatment of founders, employees, and minority shareholders

  • Balanced agreements designed for durability, not extraction

  • Negotiations conducted in good faith

Fairness is essential to sustainable partnerships and reputation.

3

A portion of our effort and success is directed toward broader societal benefit. This includes philanthropic engagement, mentorship, and support for initiatives that strengthen economic and social wellbeing.

4

  • Disciplined evaluation of operational, financial, and market risks

  • Conservative use of leverage appropriate to each business

  • Focus on resilient industries and clear value catalysts

  • Continuous monitoring throughout the investment lifecycle

Our objective is not to eliminate risk — which is impossible — but to calibrate it intelligently.

5

  • Implementation of robust board structures and oversight

  • Promotion of ethical conduct and compliance

  • Support for professional management development

  • Long-term value creation over short-term gain

Strong governance protects all stakeholders and enhances enterprise value.

6

  • Support for fair employment practices and workforce development

  • Encouragement of sustainable business operations

  • Engagement with local communities where appropriate

  • Commitment to ethical standards across the value chain

  • We believe responsible companies create more durable financial outcomes

Long-Term Partnership Mindset

We view every investment as a relationship, not merely a transaction. Our goal is to create outcomes that founders, employees, investors, and communities can look back on with confidence and pride
