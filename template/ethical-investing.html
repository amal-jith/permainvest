Ethical Investing

PermaInvest is committed to investing capital responsibly and with integrity. We will not knowingly invest in, support, or facilitate activities that cause harm to people, animals, the environment, or the broader community.

Our investment decisions are guided by the principle that financial returns must not come at the expense of human dignity, societal wellbeing, or planetary sustainability.

We do not invest in businesses or activities that:

  • Cause physical, social, or economic harm to individuals or communities

  • Exploit vulnerable populations or labour

  • Harm animal welfare through cruel or inhumane practices

  • Degrade the environment or contribute to unsustainable resource use

  • Operate in ways that are unethical, deceptive, or predatory

  • Involve illegal activities or the circumvention of laws or regulations

  • Seek to profit from regulatory loopholes that undermine public interest

  • Create material hardship, instability, or long-term damage to communities

We avoid investments where governance standards, transparency, or accountability fall below acceptable thresholds, or where reputational, social, or environmental risks cannot be responsibly mitigated.

PermaInvest actively seeks to partner with businesses that demonstrate responsible leadership, lawful conduct, and a commitment to positive impact. We believe that ethical behaviour, sound governance, and long-term value creation are inseparable.

Where concerns arise, we will engage constructively with management to address issues. If meaningful improvement cannot be achieved, we reserve the right not to proceed with or to exit the investment.

Our objective is to deploy capital in a manner that generates strong financial outcomes while contributing to a fair, sustainable, and prosperous society.
