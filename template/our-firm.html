Our Firm

We invest with conviction, partnering closely to scale resilient, high-growth businesses.

PermaInvest is built on a simple premise: while capital is abundant, truly risk-calibrated mid-market opportunities are not. We invest on a deal-by-deal basis, committing our own capital first and completing full underwriting and commercial diligence before introducing external investors.

Capital is selectively sourced from partners aligned to the sector, jurisdiction, and stage of each opportunity. We originate investments year-round through trusted advisory networks, focusing on growth industries with clear, actionable value catalysts. By deploying capital from the outset, we ensure alignment from day one. External investors access the same institutional-grade diligence, supported by flexible, tailored structuring. Our approach is disciplined and principled. We apply strict exclusion criteria and prioritise responsible stewardship across all investments. Transactions are structured as full acquisitions or staged investments, enabling founders and shareholders to transition ownership while preserving continuity, culture, and enterprise value.

PermaInvest is an active partner. We work closely with management teams to strengthen strategy, operations, and governance, and to deliver practical, execution-led value creation that supports sustainable long-term growth.

We structure transactions for speed and certainty, underpinned by pre-arranged financing and bespoke capital solutions. The originating deal team remains actively involved throughout the lifecycle, targeting ~2.0× MOIC or c.25% gross IRR over a typical three-year hold, subject to market conditions.

We partner with founders, boards, and investors to deliver outcomes defined by discipline, integrity, and aligned value creation.
